Brezovica. A serene ski destination amidst the majestic Balkan Mountains.

Brezovica is the largest ski center in Kosovo. It is located in the municipality of Štrpce in the south of the country, about 20 kilometers east of Prizren and five kilometers southwest of the main town of Štrpce.

Prishtina Mall. A shopper's paradise with something for everyone.

Prishtina Mall is a premier shopping destination in the heart of the capital city of Kosovo. With its modern and spacious design, it offers a unique shopping experience that combines fashion, entertainment, and leisure. Visitors can browse through a wide selection of local and international brands, catch a movie at the cinema, or enjoy a meal at one of the many restaurants and cafes.

Prizren. A historic landmark offering panoramic views of the old town.

The Prizren Castle is a well-preserved fortress atop a hill in Kosovo. Dating back to the 6th century, it offers visitors an impressive display of ancient walls, towers, and gates. The castle played a significant role in the region's history and provides stunning panoramic views of the town below. A must-visit for history and architecture enthusiasts.
